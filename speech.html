Greetings to you all.

l Bahle Ndhlovu would like to tell you a speech about the causes, effects and solutions to child marriage. l first presented this speech to school children and teachers,now l am going to share it with all of you so that you can know how to save girls from child marriage.

Child marriage is when a child under the age of eighteen years is forced to get married.
The causes of child marriage are poverty, dowry, religious and social noms.
The effects of child marriage are cervical cancer, early pregnancy and still birth.
We can solve child marriage by providing incentives to girls and their families and encouraging supportive laws.
l would like to say to young girls say no to child marriage.

Thank you all for taking your time to read this speech.
